## Overview

`.env-buddyicon` will always give you a square image. Use modifiers for size and form (square or round):<br>
`env-buddyicon--large`, `env-buddyicon--medium`, `env-buddyicon--small`, `env-buddyicon--x-small`, `env-buddyicon--round`
